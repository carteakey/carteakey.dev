## Archive Maze Sites

Designed with Stanley Donwood. Each is a different era. Sites 5, 6, and 11 are dead (404).

- [Site 1](http://archive.radiohead.com/Site1/) — Pablo Honey era; "Get your fix here" links page naming the earliest fansites
- [Site 2](http://archive.radiohead.com/Site2/) — The Bends era; "This is the Radiohead internet site"
- [Site 3](http://archive.radiohead.com/Site3/) — OK Computer era; "a site by radiohead and stnly dnwd who is a confused man"
- [Site 4](http://archive.radiohead.com/Site4/) — Geocities-era earnestness; a sincere link lives somewhere in here
- [Site 7](http://archive.radiohead.com/Site7/) — Kid A era; links to Friends of the Earth, internet as activism
- [Site 8](http://archive.radiohead.com/Site8/) — Donwood labyrinth; corridors of dead links and half-remembered artwork
- [Site 9](http://archive.radiohead.com/Site9/) — HTTT era; Flash labyrinth linking to radiohead.tv and slowlydownward.com
- [Site 10](http://archive.radiohead.com/Site10/) — "TEMPORARY RESTRAINING ORDER." One page. Deliberately minimal.
- [Site 12](http://archive.radiohead.com/Site12/) — In Rainbows era; "the official website where we put things" + Dead Air Space
- [Site 13](http://archive.radiohead.com/Site13/) ★ — "meaningless series of loops, discarded ephemera" — start here

## Fan Sites & Deep Archives

- [Citizen Insane](https://citizeninsane.eu/) — exhaustive database, all albums + solo projects, est. 2006, still actively updated ✅
- [Green Plastic Radiohead](https://www.greenplastic.info/) — oldest US fansite, est. 1997, frozen ~2012, deliberately preserved ✅
- [Green Plastic Cold Storage](https://www.greenplastic.info/cold-storage/) — print articles, fan art, Ed's diary, facsimile of lost site "Lift" ✅
- [At Ease](https://ateaseweb.com/) — long-running news + live archive; board died Aug 2024, news pages still up ⚠️
- [W.A.S.T.E. Central](https://wastecentral.ning.com/) — semi-official Ning fan community; decayed, accounts mostly inaccessible ⚠️
- [Radiohead Almanac](https://the-radiohead-almanac.fandom.com/wiki/The_Radiohead_Almanac_Wiki) — fan wiki cataloguing every known live recording ✅

## Dead & Lost

Sites that existed, that people used. Listed here so they're not forgotten.

- `ateaseweb.com/board` — the message board; ~20k daily visitors at its peak; died August 2024 ❌
- `mortigitempo.com` — Green Plastic's message board; more welcoming than At Ease; gone ❌
- `58hours.com` — gig database with world-map interface; the canonical setlist source for years; gone ~2022 ❌
- `msgboard.radiohead.com` — official band board; still resolves but overrun by spam ⚠️
- `followmearound.com` — the fan campaign that worked; "Follow Me Around" finally appeared on Kid A Mnesia; site gone ❌
- `injektilo.com` — early FTP live-show host, absorbed into Green Plastic ~1999 ❌ [Wayback only](https://web.archive.org/web/*/injektilo.com)
- `lift` — Jonathan Moore's early fansite; facsimile preserved in [Green Plastic Cold Storage](https://www.greenplastic.info/cold-storage/) ❌
- Planet Telex / Wasteland / FADE OUT / Tony's Page — named on Site 1's links page as the key mid-90s fan hubs; all gone ❌
- Sites 5, 6, 11 — dead 404 ❌
